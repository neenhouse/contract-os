# ContractOS -- Vision

## North Star

ContractOS eliminates the friction of managing business contracts. It replaces scattered documents, missed deadlines, and manual tracking with a single platform where contracts are created from battle-tested templates, negotiated with a clause builder, signed electronically, and monitored through expiration with proactive renewal alerts.

## Design Principles

### 1. Clarity Over Complexity
Legal documents are inherently complex. The interface must not add to that complexity. Every screen should answer one question clearly: what do I need to do next?

### 2. Trust Through Transparency
Users are managing legally binding documents. The platform must instill confidence through visible audit trails, clear status indicators, and predictable workflows.

### 3. Speed to Signature
The primary value metric is time-from-draft-to-signature. Every feature decision should be evaluated against whether it shortens or lengthens this path.

### 4. Proactive, Not Reactive
The system should surface upcoming deadlines, expiring contracts, and renewal opportunities before users have to ask. Notifications and dashboards should prevent problems, not just report them.

### 5. Composability
Templates and clauses are building blocks. Users should be able to mix, match, and customize without starting from scratch. The clause library grows more valuable with every contract created.

## Target Users

- **Small business owners** managing vendor and client agreements without dedicated legal counsel.
- **Operations managers** responsible for tracking dozens of active contracts across departments.
- **Legal teams** at growing companies who need standardized templates and approval workflows.

## Success Metrics

- Time from blank page to signed contract (target: under 15 minutes for standard agreements)
- Percentage of contracts renewed before expiration (target: 95%)
- User-created templates adopted by other users (measures composability)
- Zero missed expirations for active users
